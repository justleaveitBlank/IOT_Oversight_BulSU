warningHigh Voltage Warning:

The project operates on a High Voltage AC Power. Precautions that must be applied before handling High Voltage Equipment is required as the danger of Electric Shock evident. Use appliances that are rated below 5A for safety.

NOTE:
  1. Refer to the Manual before using the Project.
  2. The proponents do not advise disassembly of the project, delicate wirings and solders may come off and could cause a potential short circuit.
  3. Do not shake, drop, or exert physical force on the project.
  4. Do not cut, puncture, or crush this project.
  5. Keep away from fire, heat, water, oil, and other corrosive materials.
  6. The project takes up 2 to 3 minutes to fully boot, please be patient.
